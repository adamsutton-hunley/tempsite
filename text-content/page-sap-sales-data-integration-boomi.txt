SAP Sales Data Integration via Boomi
A complex integration using Boomi to streamline global sales data management for a company with operations in five countries.

Key Achievements:

Integrated Five SAP HANA Instances: Connected SAP HANA databases in five countries to two Salesforce environments using the Boomi integration platform. 

Synchronized Sales Data Nightly: Automated nightly data transfer of account, invoice, order, and product information from all SAP instances into Salesforce.

Improved Data Visibility & Reporting: Enhanced data accessibility within Salesforce, enabling generation of comprehensive sales reports across all regions.

Collaboration & Design: Collaborated with colleagues on the design and implementation of the Boomi integration, ensuring alignment with business requirements.
